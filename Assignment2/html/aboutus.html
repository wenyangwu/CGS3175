About Us

We are a team of metallurgy specialists and professional blacksmiths based in Portland, Oregon! Many of us come from technical trade backgrounds with over 10+ years of experience, and have recently decided to band together to form A Knight's Apparel. Medieval armaments have always been of interest to us, and it was a natural transition for us to progress from our day jobs to crafting historical weapons.
